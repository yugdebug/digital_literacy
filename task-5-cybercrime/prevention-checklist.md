#  Stay Safe Online:A Digital Ambassador’s Prevention Guide

I have compiled these 8 useful tips to help our community stay secure against evolving cyber threats.

### General Digital Hygiene:-
1. **Enable Multi Factor Authentication:** Secure your university email and  other social media accounts with an extra layer of protection to prevent scams.
2. **The Verify Before Click Rule:** Never click on suspicious links in emails such as Free Internship offers without verifying  who the sender is.
3. **Audit App Permissions:** Regularly check which apps have access to your camera, microphone and location.
4. **Use Strong and Unique Passwords:** Use a mix of symbols, numbers and letters. Avoid using the same password for your all accounts.
5. **Secure Your Network:** Avoid performing financial transactions or logging into sensitive portals while on public WiFi.
6. **Check for HTTPS:** Always ensure the website URL starts with `https://` before entering any personal data.

### Financial & UPI Safety;-
7. **PIN is ONLY for Paying:** Remember, you **never** need to enter your UPI PIN to receive money.
8. **Verify the Name:** Before hitting Send button on any UPI app. Always double check the verified name that appears after scanning a QR code to ensure it matches the correct recipient.
