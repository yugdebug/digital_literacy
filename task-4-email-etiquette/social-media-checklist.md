# 🛡️ Digital Ambassador's Social Media Guide

this checklist serves as a standard for college students to manage their online identity responsibly.

---

### ✅ The 5 "Do’s" for a Professional Presence
1. **Audit Your Tagged Content:** Regularly review photos and posts others have tagged you in to ensure your public profile remains recruiter friendly.
2. **Verify Before You Share:** Fact check news before reposting on social media to prevent the spread of digital misinformation.
3. **Use Privacy Segments:** Utilize Close Friends lists or private accounts for personal life.
4. **Engage with Industry Leaders:** Follow and respectfully interact with professionals in your field on LinkedIn.
5. **Enable Multi-Factor Authentication:** Secure all social media accounts with an extra layer of protection to prevent unauthorized access and identity theft.

---

### ❌ The 5 "Don’ts" for Responsible Conduct
1. **Don't Vent Publicly:** Avoid posting frustrations regarding faculty, peers or assignments as digital footprints are permanent online.
2. **Don't Overshare Real-Time Locations:** Avoid posting live at specific places to maintain your physical safety.
3. **Don't Plagiarize Digital Assets:** Never post project work or creative content created by others without providing proper credit.
4. **Don't Engage with Trolls:** Avoid public arguments that could damage your professional reputation.
5. **Don't Click Suspicious DMs:** Be wary of links as these are common entry points for phishing scams across.
